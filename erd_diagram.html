Schema Logic

Authentication

Users are split into students and tutors. Each has a UUID primary key. Tutors have additional fields for their professional profile (bio, rating, subjects).

Coordination

The bookings table is the central hub, linking students and tutors. Once a booking is created, it acts as the parent for all session interactions.

Interaction Rooms

The messages and notes tables are scoped directly to a booking_id, ensuring privacy and session-specific data.

Scheduling

Tutor availability is managed in the availability table, storing time-slot strings mapped to specific days of the week.
