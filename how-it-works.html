Frequently Asked Questions

How does the payment process work?

expand_more
Payments are handled securely through our platform. We hold the funds in escrow until the lesson is completed to ensure satisfaction for both parties.

Can I cancel or reschedule a lesson?

expand_more
Yes, you can reschedule or cancel up to 24 hours before the session starts without any penalty. After that, a small cancellation fee may apply.

What if I'm not happy with my tutor?

expand_more
We offer a 100% Satisfaction Guarantee. If you're not happy with your first lesson, we'll help you find a new tutor and cover the cost of your first hour with them.
