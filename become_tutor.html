Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about tutoring on our platform.

What qualifications do I need to apply? expand_more

We look for experts with a deep understanding of their subject matter. While a formal degree is preferred, professional experience or a strong teaching track record also carry significant weight.

How much can I earn as a tutor? expand_more

Tutors set their own hourly rates. Average rates vary by subject and experience, typically ranging from $25 to $100+ per hour. Top tutors earn over $5,000 monthly.

How and when do I get paid? expand_more

Payments are processed securely via Stripe or PayPal. You can withdraw your earnings weekly once they are cleared in your dashboard.

Is there a minimum hour requirement? expand_more

No, you have complete control over your schedule. You can teach 2 hours a week or 40. We only ask that you maintain high quality and professional communication.
